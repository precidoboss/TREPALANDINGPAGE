What is Trepa?

Trepa is a place to flex your financial foresight and get paid. Prediction markets today turn every question into a binary yes or no bet. For example: “Will job numbers be over 100,000 next month?” If the actual number was 100,001 but you guess 99,999, you lose—just like someone who guessed 50,000. We believe there should be an upside for being accurate.

Trepa is here to change that by building the world’s first precision predictions platform. With our platform, you don’t need to be right, you need to be accurate. If the correct answer for the inflation rate is 5% and you guess 4.9%, you get a big payout. If you guess 8%, you’re way off and get a smaller payout. Our mission is to make this experience extremely easy to use: simply slide, stake, and show off your IQ.
